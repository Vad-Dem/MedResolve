Surgery Funding

Surgery Funding for plaintiffs is a pre-settlement advance program for uninsured clients to get much-needed medical care from quality board certified surgeons while adding substantial value to the pending case. If you need surgery, there is no waiting and no need to be concerned with up-front payments before performing your surgery. There is also no need to be concerned if a quality surgeon does not accept your insurance plan. With a MedResolve Pre-Settlement Surgical Funding, your surgeon receives his/her fees in advance of your surgery to preserve your booked procedure.

    MedResolve provides surgical funding for all types of procedures,
    including the following:
  • Arthroscopic knee surgery
  • Arthroscopic Shoulder, Hip and Elbow Surgery
  • Ankle and Foot
  • Surgery Plastic and ENT Surgeries
  • Cervical Discectomy
  • Cervical Fusion, Single Level and Multiple Level
  • Lumbar discectomy
  • Lumbar Fusion single and multi-level
  • Lumbar laminectomy
  • Removal of Hardware

Post-Surgery Funding

MedResolve provides advances for post-surgery living expenses while you recover from a recent surgical procedure. This is seen by many attorneys as a value-add as it allows the patient to recover and gives more time to the attorney to settle the case for the highest amount possible.
