Who We Fund

Health Care Providers

MedResolve provides financing for medical providers, surgeons, surgery centers
and other health-care facilities who provide care to patients in reliance on a lien or letter
of protection (“LOP”). Rather than wait for the uncertain outcome of the underlying
personal injury case, our group of select medical providers who qualify for this type
of funding receive instant cash in exchange for the assignment
of the medical lien or the proceeds of such medical lien to MedResolve.
Medical providers who rely on this solution increase cash flow to their businesses
and normalize their revenue streams from personal injury care. Our medical lien funding solution for personal injury patients pays significantly higher reimbursements
than Medicare and many major medical insurance programs and is paid much faster than those insurance programs. Partnering with MedResolve also reduces the cost
to the provider of managing a portfolio of liens, eliminating costs and overhead associated
with verification, servicing and collections.

How Does Medical Lien Funding Work?

A medical lien funding is typically transacted as a discounted purchase of the gross
charges that the medical provider has billed to the client and secured via a lien on the
underlying personal injury case. Similar to pre-settlement funding, MedResolve
underwrites the underlying personal injury case to determine adequate coverage and
compelling damages to sustain the repayment of the bill. MedResolve then determines the
appropriate risk-adjusted discount that can be offered as immediate payment to the
health-care provider with a view to collecting a premium over such discounted purchase.
MedResolve funds liens on single cases as well as entire portfolios of liens.
